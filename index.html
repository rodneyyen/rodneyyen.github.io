Dr. Rodney Yen

Dr. Yen has a long history with the Pacific Northwest. Born and raised in Seattle, he graduated from Evergreen High School in Burien.

Redmond Podiatrist

Dr. Rodney G. Yen is a skilled board certified physician with advanced training in both conservative and surgical treatment options. He is the premier consultant for foot and ankle conditions. With over 24 years of experience and affiliations with Evergreen, Overlake, and Swedish Hospitals, Dr. Yen remains at the forefront of the latest conservative and surgical techniques.
